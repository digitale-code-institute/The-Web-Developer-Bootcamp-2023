In the previous lecture we created our farm model, but two important lines of code were left unexplained and may be easy to miss when following along.

At the very bottom of your farm.js file, please be sure to include the following lines of code:

const Farm = mongoose.model('Farm', farmSchema);

module.exports = Farm;

What we're doing here is creating and exporting the Farm model.

This makes it so we can import the model into other parts of the program, like our routes, which we'll be doing in the next lecture.
